FAQ:

What is this? What do I do with it? I'm a rider, can I leave a ride I joined? I'm a driver, how do I edit or cancel a ride? Can I help make it better?
Hitch A Ride is a simple webapp for matching drivers with riders. If you don't have a car, join one of the rides listed above, or create a request for a ride to a hike you want to do. Hopefully, someone with a car will be open to driving with you there. If you have a car, let people know where you are driving to go hiking. People without cars can join your ride and you'll get to meet some awesome hiking partners!
If you don't have a car, join a ride or create a request. 		Your driver can! Just ask them to remove you from the ride. Drivers, you can remove someone from a ride in the edit section. Click on Details > Edit to edit your ride. You'll need your secret code to access the edit page. YES!! This project is open source and you are welcome to help out!
